Heuristic Evaluation

After this last set of prototypes, each of us individually performed a heuristic evaluation of our prototypes, based on these 10 heuristics:

  • Visibility of system status
  • Match between system and the real world
  • User control and freedom
  • Consistency and standards
  • Error prevention
  • Recognition rather than recall
  • Flexibility and efficiency of use
  • Aesthetic and minimalist design
  • Help users recognize, diagnose, and recover from errors
  • Help and documentation

This evaluation really helped us think through all aspects of our app, catching small, but important details, such as the omission of a back button on some pages or lack of feedback on whether or not a preference had been selected.

Typographic Evaluation

We also collectively performed a typographic evaluation of our app, finding 25 things that we could improve upon. This helped us think through a lot of the graphical design decisions that we hadn't really taken the time to think about before, but resulted in a cluttered interface.
